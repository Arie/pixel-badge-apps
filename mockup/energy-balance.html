USE gauge — "how much solar?" indicator

Goal: see at a glance if solar ≈ usage (zero-metering). Bar/marker scaled to 6 kW.

Option A — solar marker (recommended)

Purple bar = home usage. Green tick (raised) = current solar. Marker short of the bar end → importing the gap; marker at the end → zero-metering; marker past the end → exporting.

Option B — two-tone bar

Green = solar covering the house, purple tail = grid import, green overshoot past usage = export.
